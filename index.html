A clear, structured process from the moment you reach out to the day everything goes live. No surprises, no guesswork.

Step 1 — Before We Start

No commitment, no credit card. We get on a call, I show you the AI working live on your phone, and give you an honest recommendation. If it's a good fit, we move forward. If not, no hard feelings — you walk away with no obligation and no charge.

Step 2 — Lock It In

Contract + Intake Form (50% to Start)

You sign the contract digitally and pay 50% of the setup fee to kick things off. The same day, I send you a short intake form — 15 minutes to fill out with your services, pricing, FAQs, and brand details. That's everything I need to build your system.

Then, over 5 business days:
Day 1

Dashboard + Calendar Setup

Your client dashboard, booking calendar, and automated phone system are configured and ready. You'll have a private login to see every lead and booking in real time.

Day 2

AI Chatbot Trained & Live

Your website chatbot is trained on your services, pricing, FAQs, and brand voice — then tested across real booking scenarios before going anywhere near your clients.

Day 3

Booking Automation + Reviews Live

The booking confirmation SMS, day-before reminder, missed-call text-back, and post-visit review automation are all configured, tested, and connected to your calendar and phone number.

Day 4

Website Built & Deployed

Your professional 5-page website is live on your domain with the AI chatbot embedded, Schema markup active, and analytics tracking every visit.

Day 5

Full Test + Video Handoff

Every system is tested end-to-end. You receive a full walkthrough video of everything working live — and we hop on a 20-min call to connect your Google Calendar and activate call forwarding.
