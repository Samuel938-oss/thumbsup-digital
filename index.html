How It Works

From First Call to Fully Live System — Step by Step

A clear, structured process from the moment you reach out to the day everything goes live. No surprises, no guesswork.

Step 1 — Before We Start

Discovery Call — We Listen First

No commitment, no credit card. We get on a call and I listen — your business, your problems, what you actually need. From there I tell you exactly what I'd build and what it costs. Pricing is decided on the call based on your scope, not a fixed menu. If it's a good fit, we move forward. If not, no hard feelings.

Step 2 — Lock It In

Contract + Intake Form (50% to Start)

Same day as the call, I send one email — your contract, your deposit link, and a personalized intake form built around what we discussed. Once all three are done, I start building.

Once we agree on exactly what your system needs, we move fast.

Then, over 5 business days:
Day 1

Dashboard + Calendar Setup

Your client dashboard, booking calendar, and automated phone system are configured and ready. You'll have a private login to see every lead and booking in real time.

Day 2

AI Chatbot Trained & Live

Your website chatbot is trained on your services, pricing, FAQs, and brand voice — then tested across real booking scenarios before going anywhere near your clients.

Day 3

Booking Automation + Reviews Live

Your automations go live — booking confirmations, reminders, review requests, and any other workflows built specifically for your setup. Tested and connected to your calendar and phone number.

Day 4

Website Built & Deployed

Your professional 5-page website is live on your domain with the AI chatbot embedded, Schema markup active, and analytics tracking every visit.

Day 5

Full Test + Video Handoff

Every system is tested end-to-end. You receive a full walkthrough video of everything working live — and we confirm every piece of your system is exactly what we agreed to build.

You'll hear from me directly — by text.

Throughout your 5-day build, I'll reach out via SMS if I need anything from you — and you can text me back anytime with questions. No support tickets, no waiting on email. Just a direct line to the person building your system.
