My Story

Built to Solve a Real Problem

I'm Samuel — an IT and Cybersecurity student based in Cary, NC. I built ThumbsUp Digital because I kept seeing the same pattern: small local businesses in the Triangle area doing genuinely great work, losing clients every single evening because they had no system in place after 5pm.

A med spa three miles from where I live had 47 clients in one month and zero new Google reviews. Their phone rang at 6:47pm on a Friday with someone asking about Botox — nobody answered. That person booked somewhere else. That happens every day, to almost every local service business, and most owners don't even know it.

The technology to fix this isn't new — large clinic chains have been using it for years. I built ThumbsUp Digital to make it accessible to the local businesses that compete against them. Same AI, same automation, at a price that makes sense for a growing practice.
