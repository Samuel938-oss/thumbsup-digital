Bot 03

Smart Booking & Review System

The moment a client books an appointment, Bot 03 takes over — automatically. It sends a booking confirmation SMS right away, then waits until the day before to send a reminder. No more no-shows. No more forgotten appointments.

One hour after the appointment ends, the client receives a review request: "How was your visit today? Reply 1–5." If they reply 4 or 5, they're instantly sent your Google review link. If they reply 1, 2, or 3, you get a private alert so you can follow up before anything negative goes public.

If a client doesn't reply at all, the system detects the no-response and sends a gentle follow-up or reschedule offer — so even quiet clients stay in your pipeline instead of disappearing quietly.

The result: 10 to 30 new Google reviews per month, a lower no-show rate, and zero manual follow-up on your end. It all runs automatically, on every single appointment, every single time.
