Bot 02

Missed-Call Text-Back

The moment a call goes unanswered, the system detects it and fires an automated SMS to the caller within 60 seconds. The message is warm and human-sounding — not a robot blast.

The AI continues the conversation from there: qualifying the lead, answering their question, and attempting to book the appointment — all over text, automatically. This system alone typically recovers 3–8 leads per month that would otherwise be lost forever.

✓Fires within 60 seconds of every missed call
✓Connects to your existing phone number via call forwarding
✓AI qualifies and books — zero manual follow-up needed
✓Warm, branded tone — feels like a real staff member
Bot 03

Google Review Automation

Two hours after every completed appointment, your client receives an automated SMS asking them to rate their experience from 1 to 5. No manual effort — it runs completely on its own.

If they reply 4 or 5, they're instantly sent your Google review link. If they reply 1, 2, or 3, you receive a private alert so you can reach out and resolve the issue before it becomes a public negative review. This system generates 10 to 30 new Google reviews per month for most clients.

✓Fully automated — fires 2 hours after every appointment
✓4–5 star ratings go straight to your Google review link
✓1–3 star ratings alert you privately — protect your reputation
✓10–30 new Google reviews generated per month, on average
